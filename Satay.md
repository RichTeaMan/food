# Satay

## Dependencies

* Chicken
* Peanut Butter
* Chicken Stock
* Chilli Powder
* Soy Sauce
* Garlic Clover

## Method

Marinade chicken in 2 tablespoons of soy sauce, half teaspoon of chilli powder and crushed garlic clove.

Heat peanut butter heaped tablespoon, soy sauce, chilli powder and 4 ounces of chicken stock.
